The **Partner console** is your workspace for assigned ecosystem projects after staff approval.

## Access

- URL: [Partner console](/partner-console)
- Requires a connected Solana wallet
- Available to **project admins** assigned to an ecosystem project, and to Arcademy staff

If you have no assigned projects yet, apply at [Become a partner](/partners/apply) or wait for staff to approve a pending application.

## Project picker

If you administer more than one ecosystem project, the console lists each project separately. Select a project to manage its console sections.

## Sections

### Analytics

Aggregate performance for published courses:

- Summary cards (starts, completions, quiz pass rate, badges, page views)
- Learner funnel and weekly trends
- Per-course lesson funnel and quiz diagnostics
- Markdown and CSV export

No individual learner identities, wallet addresses, or referral attribution.

### Course drafts

Collaborate on draft course content assigned to your project. Staff retains publish authority.

### Self-service

Submit source material URLs, requested course topics, and partner notes. Track intake **review status** through the workflow (Received → Published).

### Discord

Optional integration to grant Discord roles when learners complete a course. Configure when your community workflow is ready.

### Settings

Update assigned project metadata available to project admins.

## Recommended first visit

1. Open **Self-service** — confirm materials and review status
2. Check **Course drafts** when staff notifies you a draft is ready
3. After publish, use **Analytics** to understand learner engagement
