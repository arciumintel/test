# Partner handbook

Arcademy helps Arcium ecosystem projects onboard users through curated, structured courses. This guide explains how partnering works in V1.

## Who this is for

Ecosystem teams who want a trusted learning destination to link from documentation, product onboarding, Discord, or campaigns — without building and operating a full education platform themselves.

## What you get

- A professional **ecosystem project page** on Arcademy
- A **curated course** with lessons, a final quiz, and a completion badge
- **Wallet-linked badges** learners can view in their profile
- A **Partner console** for project settings, course publishing, analytics, and optional Discord integration
- **Stable URLs** you can reference from your own properties

## What Arcademy staff owns

Arcademy staff approves partner applications and can still review or intervene on content when needed. Approved partners publish their own project pages and courses within assigned project scope.
