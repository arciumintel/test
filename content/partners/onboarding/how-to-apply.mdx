title: How to apply

---

## Before you apply

Skim the [intake checklist](/partners/docs/onboarding/intake-checklist) and gather:

- Official ecosystem project name and description
- Key links (website, docs, app entry point)
- A rough course topic or onboarding goal
- Contact details for your team

## Step 1 — Connect your wallet

Partner accounts are anchored to a **Solana wallet address**. There is no email/password login.

1. Go to [Apply to partner](/partners/apply).
2. Connect your Solana wallet.
3. Your wallet will be linked to your partner account after staff approval.

## Step 2 — Submit your application

Complete the application form:

| Field | What to enter |
| --- | --- |
| Partner / team name | Your organization or protocol team |
| Contact name | Primary point of contact |
| Contact email | Where staff can reach you for intake |
| Ecosystem project name | Official name as it should appear on Arcademy |
| Project description | One or two sentences for new users |
| Source material URL | Optional link to docs, deck, or onboarding guide |
| Requested course topic | Optional focus for the first course |

## Step 3 — Wait for staff review

After submission, your application shows as **pending** in the Partner console. Arcademy staff reviews fit, completeness, and scheduling before approval.

## After approval

The Partner console unlocks for your assigned ecosystem project(s). Start in **Settings** to finish your project page, then create and publish courses under **Courses**. See the [publishing workflow](/partners/docs/onboarding/review-workflow) for the full checklist.
