## Overview

1. **Apply** — Submit a partner application with wallet connected
2. **Approved** — Staff approves your application and grants Partner console access
3. **Set up project** — Complete project details and publish your project page
4. **Create courses** — Draft lessons, quiz, and badge in the Partner console
5. **Publish** — Publish courses when readiness checks pass (no staff publish gate)
6. **Refer users** — Link from docs, onboarding, Discord, or campaigns
7. **Iterate** — Use analytics to improve content over time

Staff may still request changes or intervene on content when needed.

## Publishing checklist

Before you publish a course, confirm:

- [ ] Project page is published
- [ ] Course title and summary are clear for new users
- [ ] At least one required lesson is ready
- [ ] Final quiz has questions
- [ ] Completion badge is created
- [ ] Links, product claims, and quiz answers are accurate

## Your actions

**After application approval**

- Publish your project from **Project settings**
- Create and edit courses under **Courses**
- Publish or unpublish courses yourself when ready

**Published**

- Start referring users to your stable project and course URLs
- Monitor aggregate analytics after learners begin arriving
