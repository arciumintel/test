## Overview

1. **Apply** — Submit a partner application with wallet connected
2. **Received** — Source materials are on file
3. **In review** — Arcademy staff reviews your submission
4. **Draft created** — A course draft is ready for your factual review
5. **Partner review** — Your feedback is with Arcademy staff
6. **Approved** — Content approved; awaiting publish
7. **Published** — Live on Arcademy
8. **Refer users** — Link from docs, onboarding, Discord, or campaigns
9. **Iterate** — Use analytics and staff notes to improve content over time

Track your current status in the Partner console under **Self-service**.

## Your actions at each stage

**Received / In review**

- Keep source material URLs and notes current in the Self-service panel
- Respond promptly to staff questions about product facts

**Draft created / Partner review**

- Review draft content for **factual accuracy** (not copy style)
- Submit review feedback through the Partner console
- Flag incorrect claims, links, quiz answers, or badge criteria

**Approved**

- Await staff publish; no action required unless staff requests follow-up

**Published**

- Start referring users to your stable project and course URLs
- Monitor aggregate analytics after learners begin arriving

## Factual review checklist

Before you approve publication, confirm:

- [ ] Project name is correct
- [ ] Logo and brand assets are approved for Arcademy use
- [ ] Description is accurate for new users
- [ ] Links are current
- [ ] Course "next step" is correct
- [ ] Lessons do not overpromise product capabilities
- [ ] Privacy, security, and trust claims are accurate
- [ ] Quiz correct answers and explanations are accurate
- [ ] Badge criteria are accurate

## Typical timeline

Timelines depend on intake completeness and staff capacity. Most courses move faster when the [intake checklist](/partners/docs/onboarding/intake-checklist) is complete before staff begins drafting. Your Arcademy intake owner can share a target publish window after review starts.
