title: What is Arcademy?
description: The official learning platform for the Arcium ecosystem and how it works for learners and partners.
---

Arcademy is the **official learning platform for the Arcium ecosystem**. Learners browse products and courses publicly, connect a Solana wallet to save progress, complete ordered lessons, pass a final quiz, and earn an off-chain badge tied to their wallet.

## The problem it solves

Users often encounter fragmented documentation, technical terminology, and unclear onboarding paths when they discover Arcium or an ecosystem project. Arcademy turns that into **structured learning paths** with visible progress and credible completion signals.

## How learners experience Arcademy

1. Browse published ecosystem projects and courses (no wallet required).
2. View course details — outcomes, duration, difficulty, and badge reward.
3. Connect a Solana wallet when ready to start tracked progress.
4. Complete required lessons in order.
5. Pass the final course quiz (with retries and explanations).
6. Receive an off-chain badge award.
7. Follow the project's recommended next step (docs, app, Discord, or another course).

## Why partners care

Users who complete an Arcademy course arrive **better prepared** to use your product. You get a linkable, official destination instead of maintaining parallel onboarding content alone — plus basic signals on starts, completions, and quiz performance.

## What Arcademy is not (V1)

Arcademy V1 is a curated learning foundation, not a full partner-operated LMS. In this release, Arcademy does **not** include:

- Partner-direct publishing (staff approves all learner-facing content)
- On-chain or soulbound NFT credentials
- Paid courses or a course marketplace
- Developer sandboxes, AI-generated lessons, or multilingual content
- Advanced LMS features such as grading, comments, or leaderboards
