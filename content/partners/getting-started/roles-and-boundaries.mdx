---
title: Roles and boundaries
description: What partners do versus what Arcademy staff does in V1, including Partner console capabilities and limits.
---

V1 is **partner-assisted with staff-managed publishing**. Understanding who owns what prevents mismatched expectations during intake and review.

## Partners

| You do | You do not |
| --- | --- |
| Submit source material and project context | Publish courses or project pages directly |
| Answer factual questions from Arcademy staff | Edit unrelated ecosystem projects |
| Review drafts for **factual accuracy** | Rewrite learner-facing copy in the CMS |
| Refer users via stable Arcademy URLs | View individual learner wallets or identities |
| View **aggregate** analytics for assigned projects | Access UTM or referral attribution dashboards |

## Arcademy staff

| Staff does | Staff does not |
| --- | --- |
| Structure courses and write learner copy | Bypass your factual review before publish |
| Build quizzes, badges, and media presentation | Guarantee specific completion or referral numbers |
| Publish, unpublish, and maintain quality | Share learner PII with partners |

## Partner console (V1)

After approval, assigned project admins can use:

- **Analytics** — Funnel, trends, quiz diagnostics, and exports (aggregate only)
- **Course drafts** — Collaborate on draft course content
- **Self-service** — Submit source URLs, topics, and notes; track review status
- **Discord** — Optional role grants for course completers
- **Settings** — Update assigned project metadata

## Terminology

| Term | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| **Ecosystem project** | An Arcium-related product represented on Arcademy (use this in learner-facing context) |
| **Partner** | Your team or organization behind the ecosystem project |
| **Learner** | A user who completes courses and earns badges |
| **Badge** | Off-chain completion credential tied to a wallet |
| **Factual review** | Your confirmation that product claims, links, and quiz answers are accurate |

<Callout type="warn" title="Factual review is not copy approval">
  You confirm accuracy of product information. Arcademy may edit wording for clarity, tone, and beginner accessibility.
</Callout>

## Explicitly excluded from partner analytics

Partner reporting does **not** include individual learner or wallet data, UTM or referrer attribution tables, or referral link click counts. Metrics are scoped to published course performance and aggregate event data only.
