---
title: Why partner with Arcademy
description: Benefits of referring users to Arcademy for ecosystem project onboarding and education.
---

Refer users to Arcademy when you want **education quality without operating the platform yourself**. Arcademy presents your ecosystem project in the broader Arcium context and handles learner-facing course design on your behalf.

## Benefits

- **Official context** — Your project appears within Arcademy's trusted Arcium ecosystem learning layer.
- **Structured onboarding** — Users follow a clear path instead of piecing together docs on their own.
- **Reduced support burden** — Beginner education can cut repetitive questions in Discord, docs, and support channels.
- **Higher activation quality** — Users who finish a course are more likely to understand your product's value and next steps.
- **Measurable learning** — See aggregate starts, completions, quiz performance, and badge awards (no individual learner data).
- **Professional content** — Arcademy staff turns your source material into plain-language, beginner-friendly copy.

## Where to link Arcademy

Partners typically link from:

- Project documentation and help centers
- In-app education prompts and onboarding checklists
- Discord welcome flows and community announcements
- Blog posts, launch campaigns, and ecosystem pages

## What success looks like

A referred user discovers your course, completes lessons, passes the quiz, earns a badge, and follows your configured **next step** — visiting your app, reading docs, or joining your community with clearer context.

<Callout type="info" title="Partner-assisted, not partner-operated">
  You submit source material and review factual accuracy. Arcademy staff publishes all learner-facing content. Set this expectation with your team before you apply.
</Callout>
